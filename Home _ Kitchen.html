    DANIEL'S  KITCHEN 

    Gourmet Food Truck & Catering

    Executive Chef Daniel Sanchez leads culinary enthusiasts on an exhilarating journey through the culinary traditions of Mexico and the United States through extraordinary classics. Fueled by an unwavering passion for gastronomy, Sanchez possesses a remarkable talent for tantalizing the senses, seamlessly blending simple, yet exquisite, ingredients with innovative twists that elevate every dish to an unforgettable experience.

    FOOD TRUCK SERVICES

    For visual examples of Executive Chef Danny's food truck dishes go to the gallery.

    At Daniel's Kitchen Food Truck, Executive Chef Danny serves crispy barbacoa tacos, roasted vegan cauliflower tacos, and caramelized vegan portobello mushroom tacos. Executive Chef Danny also serves tasty burritos, delectable salads, and sweet flan desserts. Food truck operating hours are Monday through Saturday 10:30 AM-3:30 PM on 850 Woodside Road. Join us and enjoy the Mexican fusion cuisine!
